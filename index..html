FORMULA 1

Formula 1 is the pinnacle of motorsport — where engineering innovation meets sheer driver talent. When you think of speed, you think of Formula 1. Formula 1 isn’t just a sport; it’s a living, breathing testament to innovation and competition

Over the years, not only has it provided us with edge-of-the-seat entertainment, legendary rivalries, and countless memories, it has also led led to some of the most ground-breaking innovations in motorsport and automotive industry as a whole. From roaring V12s to today’s turbo-hybrid power units, the sport has seen drastic changes in speed, safety, and spectacle. Let us begin our journey to see how Formula 1 evolved into the sport it is today and some signficant events and milestones that are pivotal to this story.
